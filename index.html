About STEM CIRCUIT

Nowadays, marked by technological advancement and the Internet of Things (IoT), the fusion of hands-on electronics education and web-based connectivity has opened up exciting new horizons. Snap Circuits are a beloved educational tool for teaching electronics and propels it into the digital age. Because, Snap Circuits has gained popularity for its innovative approach to teaching electronics without requiring soldering, complex wiring, or a deep understanding of circuit theory. The snap circuits must be controlled and monitored physically using a power supply. This project primary thought is to build an IoT website for snap circuits. This web browser was built to control snap circuits from anywhere.
